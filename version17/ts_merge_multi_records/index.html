Merge Multiple Records.

"This app facilitates the merging of lead records through a straightforward two-step process, making it easy with just a few clicks."

Click on the CRM button

Click on leads button to open leads records

Select the leads record and click on actions

Click on merge records button

Select the one options of the merge type

CLICK ON KEEP ONE REMOVE OTHERS

All the selected leads appear on the screen

Click on leads to keep and select the leads record

Click on merge record

A new lead is generated

Select the leads and click on actions

Select merge records

In merge type click on custom combination

A wizard opens click on custom combination

Select the records accordingly and then click on the merge records

New Lead is generated
