Calls Feature, Auto-Format Phone Numbers & Managing Leads

Phone Calls linked with CRM

This app lets you manage Leads by their Phone Number in a few simple clicks

In the settings, go to Phone Calls and select Phone Call Manager

In CRM the Phone Calls section is visible. Click on New to create a new Lead.

Enter the details as required and then save.

Note that the phone number is automatically saved in international format.

From the Configuration menu go to Settings

In the Phone Calls section, make sure to check on CRM Lead.

This enables the Phone Calls module to fetch Lead records from CRM.

You can enter all the details including Phone Number in the form and save it.

If a Lead with the same Phone Number as entered already exists in the CRM records, it will be fetched automatically.

From the Configuration menu go to Settings and uncheck on CRM Lead and then click on Save

Note that after this step the Lead records from CRM cannot be fetched.

Click on Create Lead. This will open a wizard.

Enter the details and click on Create.

Record is created. To view all records click on Calls.

Newly created record is visible

Fetching CRM Leads by Phone Number
