Merge Multiple Records.

This application makes it easier to merge multiple CRM lead records. Users can merge records with a simple two-step process. This helps minimize the complexity involved in dealing with duplicate or redundant leads. One record can be kept, and the other is removed with just one click or the custom combination merge feature where you can choose specific data fields from both records. This efficient tool smoothes lead management and assists you in maintaining your CRM system organized and up-to-date.

"This app facilitates the merging of lead records through a straightforward two-step process, making it easy with just a few clicks."
